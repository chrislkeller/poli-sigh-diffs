Assemblymember Evan Low Endorses Ardy Kassakhian for Assembly

May 12, 2016
Ardy Kassakhian today announced that California State Assemblymember and Assistant Majority Whip Evan Low has endorsed his campaign to represent the 43rd Assembly District. The Co-Chair of the Legislative Technology and Innovation Caucus, Assemblymember Evan Low became the youngest Asian American legislator at the age of 31 to have been elected to the Assembly in 2014. Assistant Majority Whip Evan Low joins a large group of current and former members of the state legislature who have endorsed Ardy Kassakhian, including President pro Tempore of the State Senate Kevin de León, Assembly Speaker Emeritus John A. Pérez, Assembly Majority Leader Ian Calderon, Assemblymember Lorena Gonzalez, Assemblymember Jackie Goldberg (ret.) and two former Assemblymembers who represented District 43: former Majority Leader Dario Frommer and Los Angeles City Councilmember Paul Krekorian. “Ardy has been a leader on progressive issues from improving transparency and efficiency in Glendale to ensuring equality for all Californians,” stated Assembly Assistant Majority Whip Evan Low (D- Silicon Valley). “I’m looking forward to partnering with him on education issues and creating new jobs in the innovation economy. He’s the type of visionary leader we need here in the State Assembly.” Continue reading

California Teamsters Public Affairs Council Endorses Ardy Kassakhian for Assembly

May 10, 2016
Ardy Kassakhian today announced that the California Teamsters Public Affairs Council has endorsed his campaign for Assembly District 43. Representing over 250,000 Teamster members and their families across the state, the announcement follows an early endorsement for Kassakhian from Teamsters Joint Council 42. “The Fight for $15 has become a reality, but we still have a lot of work to do on behalf of California’s working families,” stated Ardy Kassakhian. “As your next Assemblymember, I will fight to keep good jobs in our state, and ensure that the people who work hard every day have the protections to do their jobs. I’m very proud to have earned the support of the California Teamsters Public Affairs Council in our efforts to fight for the working people of our great state.” Continue reading

Assemblymember Lorena Gonzalez Endorses Ardy Kassakhian for Assembly

May 09, 2016
Ardy Kassakhian today announced that California State Assemblymember Lorena Gonzalez has endorsed his campaign to represent the 43rd Assembly District. Having served in the State Legislature since 2013, Assemblymember Lorena Gonzalez is the first Latina in California history to Chair the Appropriations Committee. “During his decade of service to the residents of Glendale, Ardy has worked hard to increase civic participation, modernize the operation of the clerk’s office and increase transparency, while saving tax payer dollars,” stated Assemblymember Lorena Gonzalez. “Ardy has deep roots in the community and understands the challenges facing our state. He will make a strong advocate for the working families of the 43rd Assembly District and I am proud to support him.” Continue reading

Los Angeles Mayor Eric Garcetti Endorses Ardy Kassakhian for Assembly

May 04, 2016
Ardy Kassakhian today announced that Los Angeles Mayor Eric Garcetti has endorsed his campaign for Assembly District 43. Mayor Garcetti joins a long list of Los Angeles Democratic leaders who are backing Kassakhian’s candidacy, including L.A. Councilmembers David Ryu and Paul Krekorian, L.A. Community College Board President Scott J. Svonkin, and LAUSD Board President Steve Zimmer. The major endorsement from L.A.’s top official cements Kassakhian’s standing as the clear front-runner in the race. “Ardy Kassakhian has made a real difference in the effort to promote justice and protect voting rights, and in the State Assembly he will fight for better schools, safe neighborhoods and keeping entertainment industry jobs right here,” Mayor Eric Garcetti said. “I’m proud to add my voice to Ardy’s growing coalition of supporters.” Continue reading
