Laura Friedman Begins November Race With A Greater Than 2 To 1 Cash On Hand Advantage Over Kassakhian.

August 2nd, 2016

Laura Friedman begins November race with a greater than 2 to 1 cash on hand advantage over Kassakhian. 8/2/16 Contact: Parke Skelton 626-535-9616 Laura Friedman, who finished first by a large margin in the AD43 primary election, has a massive cash-on-hand advantage over her runoff opponent, Ardy Kassakhian. Filings released yesterday (August 1) show Friedman with $124,797 cash on hand heading into the November run-off. Kassakhian had just $60,733. In the primary, Friedman defeated Kassakhian by nearly 8,000 votes. Kassakhian devoted a great deal of effort to a vote by mail campaign but just barely edged out Friedman by 705 votes in the absentees. Friedman walloped Kassakhian in the precincts by 8,624 votes.
