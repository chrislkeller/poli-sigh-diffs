Senator Connie Leyva Endorses Laura Friedman for AD43

July 6th, 2016

Senator Connie Leyva endorses Laura Friedman in AD43 For Immediate Release 7/6/16 Contact: Parke Skelton 626-535-9616 California State Senator Connie Leyva has endorsed Laura Friedman for State Assembly in District 43. Senator Leyva is Chair of the Democratic Caucus in the California State Senate. She is the former President of United Food and Commercial Workers, Local 1428. She also served as the first woman President of the California Labor Federation. “Laura Friedman is an outstanding community leader and a strong champion for working families,” stated Senator Leyva. “Friedman helped lead the Fight for $15 in her community and brought hundreds of middle class jobs to her city.
